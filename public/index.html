Welcome!

Please read our terms of service:

Right now, this site is still in-development, so the terms of service haven't been ironed out quite yet. Basically, just don't be a jerk (i.e. don't attempt to break the site).


Some cool things you can do:

  • Create new user!
  • Logout, login, close the browser and reload the page without losing your session!
  • Do intricate searches to find any furniture you could want!
  • Add furniture to your house, or to a wishlist!
  • Furniture added to your house is automatically stored in your collection page!
  • The score for your house is calculated as items are added to rooms! View My House to check your total!
  • Ruby generates the markup for search filters so I didn't have to type it all!

Some issues to keep in mind:

  • You can't delete your account yet. Once you're here, you're here forever.
  • You can't remove items from lists.
  • A search that is too general will cause the page to freeze as a result of too many results. Pagination is needed in the future.
  • There are certain search filter options that will cause a database failure due to unescaped characters, ex. "child's room"
  • SQL Injection and session spoofing are absolutely possible. Just don't do it please.
  • Passwords are stored in plain-text, as this is just a demonstration. A real production site would encrypt the passwords.
  • Some aspects of the score calculation algorithm are unimplemented. These would require dramatically increasing the complexity of the application and its database, so they're left unimplemented for demonstration purposes.
  • The interface is... a work in progress. I wrote all of the HTML and CSS myself by hand. Please be kind.

HHA Score Algorithm

Implemented:

  • 6 furniture items in a room: 1,000 points
  • 10 furniture items in a room: 1,000 points
  • 15 furniture items in a room: 1,000 points
  • 20 furniture items in a room: 1,000 points
  • Wall-mounted furniture: 400 points x number of items (limit 3)
  • 4 or more furniture items from same series: 1,000 points x number of items
  • 3 or more furniture items from same category: 500 points x number of items
  • 70%+ of furniture in a room with same color: 200 points x number of items
  • 90%+ of furniture in a room with same color: 600 points x number of items

Unimplemented:

  • Lucky items: 777 points x number of items
  • Complete furniture set: 800 points x number of items
  • Red, green, or yellow feng shui: 500 points
